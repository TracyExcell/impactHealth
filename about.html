Impact Health the Organisation

Impact Health is an organisation the specialises in improving the quality of health and wellbeing in workplaces. We offer on the job health checks, which in turn improve the output of productivity for employers and improves the wellbeing of employees.

We Specialise in delivering:

  • Pre-employment Screening
  • Health and Wellness Screening and Monitoring
  • Hearing and Vision Testing
  • Lung Function Testing
  • Drug and Alcohol Testing - NZQA Certified
  • Flu Vaccinations
  • Chemical Testing
  • Ergonomic Workstation Assessments
  • Education and Awareness Programmes
  • Kick Butt Smoking Cessation
  • Stress Management/Counselling
  • Healthy Eating/Physical Activity
  • Diabetes and Heart Health
  • Women's and Men's Health
  • Contraception Options

Our service works with your team members to help them identify any health related issues that need to be addressed. We are trusted, independent, registered nurses and confidentiality is assured.
